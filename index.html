About Us

The high flight of 'Soch' in the literary skies—a unique blend of affordable pricing and exquisite quality.

Established with the vision of awakening a new consciousness in the realm of literature and publishing, Soch Publication House has carved a powerful niche for itself in the literary world by publishing over 100 books within just one short year of its inception. Today, several other smaller publishing firms are collaborating with Soch, functioning as unified units under its wing. The continuous support and goodwill of numerous well-wishers have been instrumental in transforming this grand mission into reality. It is our collective effort and transparent approach toward our authors that have established Soch as a deeply trusted brand.

Further expanding upon this remarkable success, the institution is all set to launch its monthly magazine in the near future. In the midst of today's intensely challenging and competitive landscape, Soch Publication House's unwavering faith and steadfast commitment to unparalleled quality continue to earn it a truly distinct identity.
